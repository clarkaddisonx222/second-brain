## Theological Focus

### Christology (The Person and Work of Christ)
- Christ as the **image of the invisible God** (1:15)
- Christ as **firstborn over all creation** (1:15)
- Christ as the **creator and sustainer** of all things (1:16-17)
- Christ as **head of the church** (1:18)
- Christ as the **reconciler** through his blood (1:20, 22)

- The church as Christ's body (1:18, 24)
- The mystery revealed to the Gentiles (1:26-27)
- Contending for one another in love (2:1-5)
- The danger of deception and hollow philosophy (2:8)

### Christian Living
- **Rooted and built up** in Christ (2:6-7)
- **Strengthened in faith** (2:6-7)
- **Overflowing with thankfulness** (2:6-7)
- **Dead to sin, alive in Christ** (2:11-13)
- **Freedom from legalism** (2:16-23)
